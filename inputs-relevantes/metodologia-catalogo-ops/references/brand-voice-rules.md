# Brand Voice Rules — MetodologIA

**Purpose:** Proteger la consistencia de marca, posicionamiento y voz en todos los documentos del catalogo. Prevenir dano reputacional por mensajes inconsistentes o claims no sustentados.

---

## 1) Identidad de Marca

### Core Positioning
- MetodologIA es un **framework metodologico** para formacion, consultoria y transformacion digital con IA aplicada
- NO es una consultora tradicional, NO es una empresa de software, NO es un curso online
- El diferenciador: **sistema repetible + practica dominante + human-in-the-loop**

### Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Means | Does NOT mean |
|-----------|-------|---------------|
| **Preciso** | Datos con unidad, condicion y fuente | Frio, distante, robotico |
| **Practico** | Cada oracion ayuda a decidir o actuar | Solo operativo sin vision |
| **Honesto** | Admite limites, condiciona resultados | Negativo, pesimista, apologetico |
| **Confiable** | Consistente entre documentos, cumple lo que dice | Rigido, inflexible, burocratico |
| **Aspiracional (solo comercial)** | Pinta un futuro mejor alcanzable | Hiperbole, promesas vacias, marketing vacio |

## 2) Prohibited Terms

### In ALL documents (canonicos, derivadas, anexos, guias)

| Term | Why prohibited | What to write instead |
|------|---------------|----------------------|
| Revolucionario | Hyperbole, unsustainable claim | "Metodo probado", "proceso guiado" |
| Disruptivo | Overused, unverifiable | "Diferenciado", "con enfoque distinto" |
| Unico | Legal risk (competitors can challenge) | "Diseñado especificamente para", "con caracteristicas propias" |
| Lider | Unverifiable market position claim | Omit or cite specific evidence |
| Innovador | Vague, unsubstantiated | Describe the specific innovation |
| Garantizamos resultados | Legal exposure | "Meta realista", "retorno estimado" |
| Sin riesgo | False — there's always risk | "Con garantia de devolucion acotada" |
| Transformacion garantizada | Outcome can't be guaranteed | "Proceso de transformacion guiada" |
| 24/7 | Unsustainable for a small team | "L-V 9-16h COT" |
| Ilimitado | Nothing is unlimited | Specific limits: "30 dias", "1 mes", "hasta 20 pax" |

### Additional prohibitions for "usuario-inexperto" documents

All terms listed in the editorial guide substitution table (ROI, OKR, KPI, SLA, framework, capstone, etc.) are PROHIBITED. Use plain-language substitutions always.

### Additional prohibitions for "canonico" and "compras" documents

| Term | Why | Instead |
|------|-----|---------|
| Increible, genial, fantastico | Marketing adjectives in technical docs | Remove; let facts speak |
| Mas de X (when exact number exists) | Imprecision | Use exact number: "100 prompts" not "mas de 90 prompts" |
| Muy, realmente, totalmente | Intensifiers that add nothing | Remove |

## 3) Required Elements by Document Type

### Every canonical MUST include:
- "Que NO es" section (Sec 2) — differentiating from adjacent services
- Human-in-the-loop statement in ethics/limits section
- Traceability tags (>=15: [EVIDENCIA], [SUPUESTO], [DECISION], [POR CONFIRMAR])
- First mention contextualization for every glossary term
- Exact prices with COP, conditions, and IVA notation

### Every comercial-cliente MUST include:
- Pain point hook (first sentence identifies a recognizable pain)
- "Es para ti/su equipo si..." section (inclusion criteria)
- "No es para ti/su equipo si..." or equivalent exclusion
- Comparativa honesta (vs alternatives including "no hacer nada")
- CTA with specific next step

### Every usuario-inexperto MUST include:
- Zero technical jargon (all substituted)
- "Conoces esta situacion?" empathy section
- "Mitos que puedes tener" objection handling
- Glosario rapido at the end
- Max 15 words per sentence

## 4) Positioning Guardrails

### What MetodologIA IS (approved claims)
- Framework metodologico de formacion y consultoria con IA aplicada
- Metodo basado en practica guiada (70% del tiempo)
- Sistema repetible que instala capacidades propias en el participante
- Enfoque human-in-the-loop: la IA propone, el humano decide
- Escalera de valor: entrada → profundizacion → premium → transformacion

### What MetodologIA is NOT (never claim or imply)
- Una empresa de tecnologia que desarrolla IA propia
- Un curso online autodirigido
- Una consultora que hace el trabajo por el cliente (done-for-you)
- Un reemplazo del juicio profesional del participante
- Una certificacion con acreditacion academica oficial
- Un proveedor de automatizaciones productivas (APIs, RPA, integraciones)

### Competitive positioning rules
- NEVER name specific competitors
- Compare against CATEGORIES: "curso online", "consultoria done-for-you", "tutoriales dispersos", "no hacer nada"
- Always include "no hacer nada" as a comparison column (cost of inaction)
- Use ranges for competitor pricing, never specific prices
- Frame comparisons as "honesta" — acknowledge where alternatives might be better

## 5) Concept Positioning (Ruta de (R)Evolucion)

### Empresarial → Soberania Digital
- The organization controls its technology decisions
- Doesn't depend on a single vendor
- Has its own criteria for evaluating, adopting, and governing digital tools including AI

### Personal → Empoderamiento
- The individual has tools, method, and clarity to direct their career
- Autonomy with AI as leverage, not crutch
- Productivity + agency + professional identity

### Cross-cutting → Amplificacion
- Using AI and repeatable systems to multiply productive capacity
- WITHOUT increasing work hours
- Human maintains criteria and decision-making
- NOT full automation; NOT replacement

## 6) Verification Checklist for Brand Consistency

Before releasing any document, verify:

- [ ] No prohibited terms present (Section 2 above)
- [ ] Voice attributes match document type (precise for canonical, aspirational for commercial)
- [ ] "Que NO es" clearly differentiates from adjacent services
- [ ] Human-in-the-loop principle stated or implied in ethics context
- [ ] Prices follow format rules (COP, dots for thousands, conditions, IVA)
- [ ] Credito educativo conditions complete (%, window, non-transferable)
- [ ] No claims that exceed canonical scope
- [ ] Comparisons are against categories, not named competitors
- [ ] CTA is specific and actionable (not generic "contactenos")
- [ ] First mention of glossary terms is contextualized for audience
- [ ] Spanish is Latin American (Colombia): "tu" not "vos", "computador" not "ordenador"
- [ ] Gender-inclusive pragmatic: "participante", "persona", "equipo" — no forced splits
- [ ] Zero emojis
- [ ] Exclamation marks within limits (0 for canonical/ejecutiva/compras; max 1 for comercial/inexperto)
