# Legal & Commercial Safety Checklist

**Purpose:** Protect MetodologIA and its commercial team from over-committing, legal exposure, and reputational risk in every document produced.

**Principle:** Un texto atractivo y vendedor NO requiere promesas absolutas. La credibilidad vende mas que la hiperbole. Cada oracion debe poder sostenerse ante un cliente insatisfecho, un abogado o un regulador.

## 1) Guarantee & Refund Safety

| # | Check | RED if... | Safe Pattern |
|---|-------|-----------|--------------|
| G-01 | Refund window is specified | "En cualquier momento", "sin limite de tiempo" | "Antes de completar las primeras {X} horas" |
| G-02 | Refund condition is specified | "Sin preguntas", "incondicional" | "A cambio de {X} hora(s) de retroalimentacion estructurada" |
| G-03 | Refund percentage matches canonical | Different % than canonical | Exact match: "Devolucion del {X}%" |
| G-04 | No "satisfaction guarantee" language | "Garantia de satisfaccion", "satisfaccion total" | "Devolucion del {X}% si no cumple expectativas" (subjective OK because bounded by time) |
| G-05 | B2B2B guarantee defers to contract | B2B2B doc promises direct guarantee | "Segun contrato marco" |

## 2) Results & Outcome Claims

| # | Check | RED if... | Safe Pattern |
|---|-------|-----------|--------------|
| R-01 | No absolute outcome guarantees | "Garantizamos resultados", "resultado asegurado" | "Meta realista: {outcome} o mas" |
| R-02 | Conditional language present | "Vas a lograr X" without condition | "Lo que logran quienes aplican el sistema con consistencia" |
| R-03 | Metrics are framed as estimates | "30% de mejora" stated as fact | "Retorno estimado", "reduccion de ~30%" |
| R-04 | Measurement responsibility is clear | Implies MetodologIA measures/guarantees the metric | "La medicion corresponde al area contratante. MetodologIA entrega plantillas de seguimiento." |
| R-05 | No "transformacion garantizada" | Any combination of transformation + guarantee | "Proceso de transformacion guiada" (process, not guaranteed outcome) |
| R-06 | Time-to-result is bounded | "Resultados inmediatos" | "Verificado en semana 3-4 post-cierre" |
| R-07 | Testimonial framework, not fabricated quotes | Quotes without attribution or made up | "[Espacio para testimonial post-ejecucion]" with capture structure |

## 3) Pricing & Financial Claims

| # | Check | RED if... | Safe Pattern |
|---|-------|-----------|--------------|
| P-01 | Prices include IVA notation | Price without IVA reference | B2B: "COP X + IVA"; B2C: "COP X (impuestos incluidos segun pasarela)" |
| P-02 | Price conditions are complete | "COP 12.000.000" without context | "COP 12.000.000 + IVA por ciclo (hasta 20 participantes)" |
| P-03 | Credit educativo conditions complete | "100% acreditable" without window/transferability | "100% acreditable, vigencia 6 meses, intransferible" |
| P-04 | No ROI guarantees | "ROI garantizado", "se paga solo" | "Retorno estimado basado en reduccion de retrabajo" |
| P-05 | Payment terms match canonical | Different installment structure | Exact match with canonical Sec 12 |
| P-06 | Exchange rate is documented if USD mentioned | USD price without rate reference | "Tasa de referencia: COP {X} = USD 1" |
| P-07 | "Gratis" is not used for paid services | "Sesion gratis", "modulo gratuito" for included items | "Incluido sin costo adicional" |

| # | Check | RED if... | Safe Pattern |

## 9) Liability Boundaries

| # | Check | RED if... | Safe Pattern |
|---|-------|-----------|--------------|
| L-01 | No unlimited liability | "Responsabilidad total", "nos hacemos cargo de todo" | Explicit limitation clause in compras/B2B2B docs |
| L-02 | Force majeure acknowledged | Promises regardless of circumstances | "Sujeto a condiciones fuera de control razonable" |
| L-03 | AI output disclaimer present | Implies AI outputs are always correct | "Human-in-the-loop: la IA propone, el humano valida y decide" |
| L-04 | Partner liability bounded in B2B2B | MetodologIA liable for partner actions | "MetodologIA no sera responsable por: (a) modificaciones no autorizadas, (b) facilitacion deficiente del partner..." |

## 10) Comparative Claims

| # | Check | RED if... | Safe Pattern |
|---|-------|-----------|--------------|
| X-01 | Comparisons are factual, not disparaging | "Mejor que [competitor name]" | Compare against CATEGORIES: "curso online", "consultoria done-for-you", "no hacer nada" |
| X-02 | "Honesta" framing present | Hidden biases in comparison table | Label as "Comparativa honesta" + include "no hacer nada" column |
| X-03 | Price comparisons are ranges, not specific | "Coursera costs $50" | "COP 50.000-300.000" (range, no named competitor) |

## 11) Self-Care Rules (protecting MetodologIA team)

These rules protect the team from burnout, operational overload, and scope creep:

| # | Rule | Why it matters | How to enforce in documents |
|---|------|---------------|---------------------------|
| SC-01 | Never commit to customizations without scope | "Contextualizamos todo a tu medida" creates infinite scope | "Contextualizacion incluida: ajuste de ejemplos, casos y ejercicios al sector del cliente" (bounded) |
| SC-02 | Never promise response times you can't sustain | "Respuesta inmediata" burns out the team | Use documented SLAs only: "Email D+1, WhatsApp <=2h (L-V 9-16h COT)" |
| SC-03 | Never accept undefined deliverables | "Lo que necesites" creates scope creep | Every entregable must be listed in canonical Sec 7 |
| SC-04 | Protect facilitator preparation time | "Podemos hacerlo manana" | "Lead time minimo: {X} dias habiles" (per service type) |
| SC-05 | Cap participant count per facilitator | "Podemos meter a 50 personas" | "Hasta 20 participantes por facilitador. >20: cofacilitacion" |
| SC-06 | Bound post-service support | "Soporte indefinido" | "Repositorio: 30 dias. Campus: {X} mes(es). Soporte post-cierre: via touchpoints programados" |
| SC-07 | Separate "included" from "extra" | Blurring lines creates expectation debt | Every service doc must distinguish "incluido" vs "con costo adicional" |
| SC-08 | Don't let urgency override quality | "El cliente lo necesita ya" | "El servicio se prepara con el lead time documentado. Ejecuciones urgentes con recargo del {X}%" |
| SC-09 | Document what you DON'T do | Client assumes you do everything | Every canonical must have "Que NO es" section (Sec 2) |
| SC-10 | Protect intellectual property always | "Te doy los prompts para que los uses con tu equipo sin nosotros" is IP leak | "Los materiales son para uso del participante. Redistribucion requiere acuerdo" |

{count} items verificados sin hallazgos.
